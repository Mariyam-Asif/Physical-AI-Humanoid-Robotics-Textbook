# References and Further Reading

This section provides a comprehensive list of references, academic papers, books, online resources, and other materials cited throughout the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. Adhering to APA style, these references ensure proper attribution and offer pathways for deeper exploration of the topics covered.

## General Robotics & AI

*   **Russell, S., & Norvig, P. (2010).** *Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (3rd ed.)*. Prentice Hall. (Comprehensive overview of AI)
*   **Siciliano, B., Khatib, O. (Eds.). (2008).** *Springer Handbook of Robotics*. Springer. (Detailed resource on various robotics fields)
*   **Thrun, S., Burgard, W., & Fox, D. (2005).** *Probabilistic Robotics*. MIT Press. (Foundational text on state estimation and mapping)

## Robot Operating System (ROS & ROS 2)

*   **Quigley, M., Conley, K., Gerkey, B., Faust, J., Foote, T., Saito, J., ... & Smith, R. (2009, May).** *ROS: an open-source robot operating system*. In ICRA workshop on open source robotics (Vol. 3, No. 12, p. 5). (Original paper introducing ROS)
*   **The ROS 2 Documentation**. (Current year). Retrieved from [https://docs.ros.org/en/foxy/](https://docs.ros.org/en/foxy/) (Official documentation for ROS 2)

## Digital Twins & Simulation (Gazebo, Unity)

*   **Koenig, N., & Howard, A. (2004, September).** *Design and use of the Gazebo simulator*. In 2004 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS) (Vol. 3, pp. 2149-2154). IEEE. (Paper on Gazebo simulator design)
*   **Unity Technologies. (Current year).** *Unity User Manual*. Retrieved from [https://docs.unity3d.com/Manual/](https://docs.unity3d.com/Manual/) (Official Unity documentation)

## AI Robot Brains (NVIDIA Isaac)

*   **NVIDIA. (Current year).** *NVIDIA Isaac SDK Documentation*. Retrieved from [https://docs.nvidia.com/isaac/sdk/index.html](https://docs.nvidia.com/isaac/sdk/index.html) (Official NVIDIA Isaac SDK documentation)

## Vision, Language, Action (VLA)

*   **OpenAI. (2023).** *GPT-4 Technical Report*. arXiv preprint arXiv:2303.08774. (General reference for large language models, relevant to VLA)
*   **Fei-Fei, L., & Perona, P. (2005).** *A Bayesian approach to unsupervised learning of object categories from object and scene fragments*. In IEEE Computer Society Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR 2005) (Vol. 2, pp. 1017-1024). IEEE. (Early work on computer vision, foundational for VLA)
